Min-List

Welcome to our minimalist todo list web app! Our app is designed to help you stay organized and focused on your tasks without any distractions. With a simple and intuitive user interface, you can easily create and manage your to-do list without any unnecessary bells and whistles.

Our app is perfect for anyone who wants a clean and uncluttered interface to keep track of their tasks. Whether you're a busy professional, a student with a lot on your plate, or just someone who wants to stay on top of their daily tasks, our app is the perfect solution for you.

Creating a new task is as easy as typing in a short description and pressing the "Add" button. You can then mark tasks as completed, delete them, or simply leave them on your list until you're ready to tackle them.

With our minimalist design, you can focus on what's truly important - your tasks. Say goodbye to distracting graphics, animations, and unnecessary features. Our app is all about helping you stay organized and productive with the least amount of effort.
